Block Dodge

How to play

Use the arrow keys to move the player around the screen. The aim of the game is to avoid the blocks for as long as possible. The blocks will speed up over the course of the game, so be careful!

Powerups

There are 4 powerups in the game, each with their own unique effect:

Turbo mode - Speeds up the player.

Slow Mo - Slows down the all the blocks.

Shield - Makes the player invincible to hitting 1 block.

Bomb - Destroys blocks in a radius around the player.

Controls

Left Arrow / A - Move left

Right Arrow / D - Move right

Up Arrow / W - Move up

Down Arrow / S - Move down

1 - Turbo mode

2 - Slow Mo

3 - Shield

4 - Bomb
